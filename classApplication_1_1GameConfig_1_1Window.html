◆ Borderless

ConfigValue<bool> Application::GameConfig::Window::Borderless = ConfigValue<bool>("borderless", false)

Borderless mode.

It removes window borders. For fullscreen mode instead of going into traditional exclusive fullscreen mode it goes into fake fullscreen mode. Fake fullscreen mode reposition window top-left corner to 0,0 ignoring window position coordinates. And also resize window to display size.
